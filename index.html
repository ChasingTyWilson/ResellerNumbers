📊 How to Download Your eBay Reports

Trying to find the right report? Here's exactly what you need to do.

Head to your eBay Reports page:

🔗 https://www.ebay.com/sh/reports/downloads

📈 To download your ORDERS from this year:

  1. Click "Download Report"
  2. Select Report Source → Orders
  3. Select Report Type → Paid and Shipped
  4. Select Date Range → This Year

📦 To download your CURRENT ACTIVE LISTINGS:

  1. Click "Download Report"
  2. Select Report Source → Listings
  3. Select Report Type → All Active Listings

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Make sure to download CSV format (not Excel)
  • Orders report gives you sales analytics and performance data
  • Listings report gives you current inventory and pricing data
  • Both reports are needed for complete analytics
